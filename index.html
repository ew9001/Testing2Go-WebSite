About Us

Founded in 2017, Testing2Go is a New York based technology company made up of passionate and dedicated testing professionals who love to test software. We also work with fast-moving clients in a wide range of industries with even a wider range of requirements and needs.

Our mission is to bring excellence to the software testing industry and deliver superior results for our clients.

What We Do

Testing2GO offers software testing for desktop, mobile apps, web apps, and automation that:

Reduce Costs

If too many customers experience downtime or issues with an app or website, they’ll be more apt to stop using the product and never use any product released by the company in the future. Potential customers see the bad press usually in the form of complaints – both online and offline – and won’t choose the product over others because of it.

These are the real costs of not fully testing their products. We find these issues before your customers do.

Increase bug detection rates

Detecting defects is of utmost importance to us, as they can affect the effectiveness and functionality of that software.

Our testers play a key role by executing well planned testing activities, which include various testing types, such as, system testing, end to end testing, regression testing, exploratory testing and more.


Improve Time to Market

One of the biggest bottlenecks for application delivery teams is the long testing cycles that occur near the end of an iteration, sprint or release.

We incorporate exploratory testing earlier in the software development lifecycle, which allows teams to find bugs earlier, identify candidates for test automation and streamline QA processes.
